The Chess PlayerBase

About The PlayerBase:

The Chess PlayerBase was created in May 2020, by two close friends named Alex and Michael. Their goal was to make a database detailing the achievements of all chess players, past and present. This database would be completely run, edited, and kept up-to-date by its community; many selfless volunteers willing to give up hours to preserve chess history for the next century.

As of July 2020, there have already been 500+ registered users with 21 player biographies fully written. There will be more coming in the near future.

We are completely ad-free and community-funded. Without our gracious supporters, this website would not be possible. Thank you all!
